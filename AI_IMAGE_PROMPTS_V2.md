## 六、关键风格参数

### 色彩方案
```
Warm tones: 5500K
Natural food colors: NO artificial-looking bright colors
Paris bakery: Warm wood + white + natural food colors
Health focus: Fresh, clean, appetizing
Business success: Professional yet approachable
```

### 禁止
```
❌ Black and white filters
❌ High contrast dramatic lighting
❌ "Intangible cultural heritage" elements
❌ Artistic abstract compositions
❌ Traditional/cliché Asian motifs
```

### 必须
```
✅ Natural appetizing colors
✅ Warm inviting lighting
✅ Clean Parisian bakery aesthetic
✅ Business success atmosphere
✅ Healthy wholesome vibe
✅ Modern professional presentation
```
